hey, I'm Joe

your desktop companion. kinda nosy,
but in a helpful way. what's your name?

👋

I'm a little character that lives on your desktop.
I float on top of everything and I'm always here.

You'll see me in the bottom-right corner of your screen. I'll pop up speech bubbles when something happens, and you can interact with me in a few ways. Let me walk you through them.
🔍

quick ask

I can see your screen and answer questions

How to use it
Hold ⌘ and click on me. A text field will appear — type your question and hit enter. I'll screenshot your screen and use AI to answer.
"Where's the export button in this app?" or "What does this error message mean?" — anything about what's on screen.
📂

file organizer

I watch your Downloads and Desktop folders

1
You download a file (a PDF, an image, anything). I notice it right away.
2
I show you a list of your projects. You pick which one the file belongs to.
3
I move the file into that project's "To Organize" folder. Done — no more messy Downloads.
You need to set up your projects first — next step shows you how.
⚙️

the menu bar

your control center for Joe

See the small yellow dot in your menu bar at the top of the screen? That's me. Click it to open the menu with all the options.
Setting up projects
Click the yellow dot → Manage Projects. Type a project name, click Add, and pick the parent folder. I'll create a "To Organize" subfolder inside it automatically.
You can also re-open this tutorial anytime from the menu.
💬

a few more things

other ways to interact with me

Click me → Chat with Claude
A regular click on me opens an AI chat. Ask anything — write emails, brainstorm, get help with work.
Double-click → Message a friend
Double-click on me to send a quick message to another Joe user. They'll see it in their speech bubble until they dismiss it.
Shortcuts
⌃ + A shows your next calendar event. ⇧ + Click sends a trillo (nudge) to the other user.
